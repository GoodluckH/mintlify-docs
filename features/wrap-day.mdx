## What is this?

Done for the day? Click on the "Wrap Day" icon in the
[List](/features/list) view to wrap up your day.

## What does it do?

When you wrap up your day, you'll see analytics of what you have done
today.

## What can I do with it?

You can use this feature to reflect your day. Use the analytics to help
you make better productivity decisions tomorrow.

There's also a "Plan Next Day" button that allows you to manage tasks
for tomorrow.

This feature also provides psychological effects by giving you a sense
of accomplishment. It's a great way to end your day.
